# Simple Future Tense

Bu tense iki başlıkta incelenebilir. Kullanımına göre:

- **will + V(1)**
- **be going to + V(1)**

şeklinde olabilir. Aralarında ufak da olsa bazı farklılıklar vardır. Ancak bu iki yapı, çoğu zaman birbirlerinin yerine kullanılabilir.

> İngiliz İngilizcesi'nde **will/won't** yerine **shall/shan't** kullanılabilir.

## will + V(1)

### Announcing Decisions At The Time Of Speaking

- I'm hungry. I'll cook something for myself.
- I've lost my wallet. I'll search for it.
- I can't do my homework. I'll get some help.

### Promises

- I won't tell anyone about our secret.
- I promise I'll take my medicines regularly.
- I promise I won't let you down!

### Threats

- They'll pay for this!
- You'll regret this!
- If he comes here again, I'll turn his head into pie!

### Requests

- Will you carry this for me?
- Will you open the window please?
- Will you help me do my homework?

### Giving Information About The Future

- The wedding will take place next Monday.
- The guests will be here in a couple of minutes.
- She'll graduate in 2 years.

## be going to + V(1)

### Intentions

- I'm going to resign from my job next week.
- Sorry I can't come. I'm going to attend the committee.
- I'm coming! I'm going to pay money to the cashier in a moment.

### Strong Predictions

- Look out! The books are about to slip from your hands.
- Slow down! You're going too fast.
- She's pregnant. She's going to give birth to twins.

> [!TIP]
> Olacağına dair elimizde kesin kanıt olmayan tahminler için iki yapıyı da kullanabiliriz.

- Man will set/is going to set foot on Mars one day.
- Turkiye will have/is going to have one of the strongest economies in the years to come.
- Oh you poor baby! It's suffering will end/is going to end soon.

## Future In The Past

Geçmişte yapması planlanan ama gerçekleşmeyen eylemler için kullanılır.

- I was going to visit you, but I had to go back.
- We were going to win the match, but they won instead.
- I knew that he would call the police.
